About the Owner

I'm the owner and artist behind Social M. Serenity, a Black‑owned studio built on color, culture, and clarity. For the past three years I've balanced creativity with business discipline — clear scopes, reliable timelines, and files that are ready for web, social, and print. I create like a storyteller and deliver like a project manager.

My purpose is bigger than pretty visuals. I want to bring fun, expressive art into millions of homes and share digital guides that build confidence — so you can ace interviews, land the roles you deserve, and communicate with power. If you value bold work with intention behind it, you’re in the right place.
